Health & Wellness Tips for a Better Life

Your health is your greatest wealth. Regular check-ups can prevent many serious health issues. Here are some essential health tips:

  • Eat a balanced diet rich in fruits, vegetables, and proteins.
  • Stay hydrated — drink at least 8 glasses of water daily.
  • Exercise at least 150 minutes a week.
  • Get enough sleep — 7 to 8 hours nightly.
  • Avoid smoking and excess alcohol.
  • Manage stress through meditation or hobbies.
  • Schedule regular health screenings especially if you have family history.
  • Maintain a healthy weight through diet and exercise.
  • Practice good hygiene to prevent infections.
  • Stay up-to-date with vaccinations and health check-ups.

❓ Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How do I prepare for fasting tests?

A: Usually, fasting for 8-12 hours is needed. Avoid fatty foods, alcohol, and heavy meals. Drink plenty of water. Take your regular medications unless advised otherwise by your doctor. Avoid smoking during the fasting period.

Q: How fast do I get my reports?

A: Routine tests are ready within 12-24 hours, critical tests within 4-6 hours, and specialized tests may take 48-72 hours. You'll receive an SMS notification when your reports are ready, and they can be accessed through our online portal.

Q: Are your labs certified?

A: Yes, all partner labs are NABL & ISO certified. We maintain strict quality control measures and participate in external quality assurance programs to ensure the accuracy of our results.

Q: Can I access reports online?

A: Yes, reports are shared via email/WhatsApp or accessible on our secure portal. You can download and print your reports anytime. Our portal also maintains your test history for future reference.

Q: Do you serve remote villages?

A: Yes, we extend home sample collection to villages within 50 km radius. For areas beyond this radius, we offer special collection days where our mobile phlebotomy unit visits the area periodically.

Q: Can I book for multiple family members?

A: Absolutely! We offer family packages and group bookings at discounted rates. Our technicians can collect samples from all family members in a single visit to minimize inconvenience.

Q: How do you ensure safety during sample collection?

A: Strict hygiene protocols, PPE, and sterilization are maintained at all times. We use single-use, disposable equipment for all procedures. Our technicians undergo regular health checkups and follow all infection control guidelines.

Q: What payment methods do you accept?

A: We accept cash, credit/debit cards, UPI payments, and online bank transfers. For corporate clients, we also provide invoice-based billing with credit facilities.

Q: Do you provide doctor consultation?

A: Yes, we offer teleconsultation services with our panel of doctors who can help interpret your reports and provide medical advice. This service is complimentary with our health packages.
