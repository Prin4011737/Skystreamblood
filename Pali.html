Skystrem Rakta Parikṣā Seva - Bhilwara meṁ Vishvasīya Rakt Parīkṣā tathā Parīkṣā

Ham Bhilwara tathā aspasta sthāno meṁ uttam darje ke rakta parīkṣā tathā swasthya jānch seva pradan karte hain. Hamare advanced lab, kushal karmchari, aur mitrata poorn sevā tezi se satya aur sahi parinaam dete hain.

Bhīlvaṛā meṁ hamāre chune hue karṇīye kyā hain?

  • Pramāṇik tathā Sahi Parinaam: NABL pramāṇit lab, kathin manako ke anusar.
  • Adhunik Upkaran: Naye takneek ke sātha satik diagnosis.
  • Visheshagya Dal: Anubhavi doctor, technician, aur swasthya karmchari.
  • Vistara Parīkṣā: 200 se adhik parīkṣā, samanya se vishesh aur adhunik.
  • Ghar se Sampala Sankalan: Aaramdayak aur surakshit sample uthāne ki seva.
  • Saste Daam & Bima Sahayata: Pratispardha mulya aur bima ke anukul seva.
